Let's solve climate change faster, together.

The Sacramento Urban Technology Lab is piloting a co-creation process to increase Sacramento's civic innovation capacity and accelerate progress toward the region's climate goals.

Exploring civic tech solutions that address climate change

By supporting the work of the Mayors' Commission on Climate Change, the Sacramento Civic Innovation Challenge will engage residents, experts, stakeholders, and technologists in a co-creation process to source and prioritize challenges that can be addressed through the application of data, technology, and user experience design.

Note: The focus of the Sacramento Civic Innovation Challenge may evolve prior to the January 31 Ideas Competition submission deadline, but we are proposing an initial challenge to structure the co-creation process.
